🔍 How to Zoom & Adjust Axes

  • Mouse Wheel / Trackpad Pinch: Hover your mouse cursor over the chart plot, and scroll up or down. The chart will zoom into the time window (1-min, 3-min, 5-min intervals) automatically!
  • Drag to Zoom: Click and hold on the chart, then draw a rectangular selection box over the specific candles or time region you want to zoom into.
  • Axis Click & Drag: Hover your cursor over the X-axis (timestamps) or Y-axis (price/strikes), and drag your mouse to contract or expand the timescale or price levels.
  • Reset View: Double-click anywhere inside the plot window to reset the zoom to default.
