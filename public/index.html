📖 TRACE Market Guide

Gamma Lens (Realized Vol)

Anticipates local areas of realized volatility based on market maker options positioning.

Blue Zones: Positive Gamma (Vol Dampens / Pin)
Red Zones: Negative Gamma (Vol Expands / Run)

Delta Pressure (Hedging Flows)

Displays net change in options delta positioning. Shows where hedging creates directional buying/selling pressure.

Positive Env: Red = Resistance / Blue = Support
Negative Env: Blue = Fuel/Break / Red = Flush/Slide

Charm Pressure (Time Decay)

Depicts exposure change with respect to time decay. Extremely critical around large 0DTE volume expirations.

Pinning Effect: Occurs near positive gamma nodes at EOD
Red/Blue Boundary: Spot price naturally drifts to boundary
